## Overview

Career Metrics AI is a career intelligence and execution platform designed to help users make informed, data-driven career decisions.

Unlike traditional tools that only provide recommendations, this system enables users to simulate career outcomes, follow structured learning paths, validate their progress, and transition into relevant job opportunities.

It focuses on transforming career planning into a continuous, verifiable, and outcome-oriented process.

---

## Problem Statement

Students often rely on fragmented resources and generic guidance. As a result:

* Career decisions are made without understanding long-term impact
* Learning paths lack structure and continuity
* Course completion does not guarantee real skill acquisition
* There is no clear transition from learning to job opportunities

---

## Solution

Career Metrics AI addresses these challenges by integrating the entire workflow into a single system:

decision → structured learning → verification → career insights → job opportunities

This ensures that users not only choose a path but also execute it effectively and reach tangible outcomes.

## Core Algorithms and Logic

### Recommendation Engine

A rule-based system that maps user inputs such as domain, interests, and difficulty level to structured career paths.

---

### Dynamic Roadmap Generation

Generates step-by-step learning paths based on selected domain and user preferences, with support for real-time updates and path switching.

---

### Verification-Based Completion

Ensures that tasks are marked complete only after submission and validation of proof or project links.

---

### Progress Calculation

Progress is calculated dynamically based on verified completed tasks relative to total tasks.

---

### Career Simulation Logic

Uses heuristic models and weighted factors to estimate salary growth, demand trends, competition levels, and risk.

---

### Job Mapping Logic

Maps acquired skills and completed modules to relevant job roles using domain-based matching.

---
